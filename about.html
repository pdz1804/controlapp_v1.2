Our Key Message

A HEALTHIER JOINTS A FAMILY JOY

AIJ not only serves as a valuable at-home assistant for osteoarthritis patients, but it also goes beyond that by offering a unique and practical gift option for elderly family members. With its innovative features and advanced technology, AIJ provides personalized care and support, enhancing the well-being and quality of life for both patients and their loved ones. The thoughtful gesture of gifting AIJ demonstrates a deep level of care and consideration, creating a meaningful and impactful experience that strengthens the bond between buyers and their elderly family members.

Purpose

The "Artificial Intelligent Joints" project is a groundbreaking endeavor aimed at assisting individuals who experience functional limitations in their limbs, particularly those related to rotational movement around the joints. With a keen focus on developing a product that caters specifically to the upper limbs, especially the arms, our project seeks to address the pressing needs of this particular demographic.

Functional

AIJ's primary function revolves around the application of supportive force to the elbow joint, facilitating more comfortable joint movements for users, thereby contributing to their overall well-being and enhanced quality of daily life. Additionally, through the integration of an AI model, AIJ gains the capacity to dynamically modify the magnitude of the supportive force in response to the individual patient's current condition. Moreover, AIJ maintains comprehensive records of patients' conditions, allowing healthcare professionals such as doctors and therapists to make informed treatment adjustments based on the patients' rehabilitation progress.

Emotional

With its innovative features and user-friendly design, this medical product becomes more than just a solution for managing osteoarthritis symptoms. It becomes a thoughtful and meaningful present that demonstrates care and consideration for the elderly family members' health and comfort. This dual functionality makes it an ideal choice for individuals seeking a gift that goes beyond conventional offerings and truly makes a difference in the daily lives of their loved ones.
